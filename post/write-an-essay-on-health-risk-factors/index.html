Write an essay on health risk factors

2023-08-31

作文要求

Write an essay of no less than 200 words to state your view . You are supposed to give a title to your essay and support your argument with specific evidence.

我的作文

Factors that Influence Our Well-being

Currently, citizens attach more and more importance to maintaining their health. Frankly speaking, it is a great pity that many have not been taught about the potential factors that could affect our health, however. Reserving some knowledge about health risk factors will enable us to do better in preventing our body from illness.
Our body is affected by several factors, the most crucial ones of which is our lifestyle as well as our mind. For one thing, if you want to avoid sickness, you should never excuse yourself from forming good habits. Here are the examples - brushing your teeth at least twice a day can keep your teeth always bright, while washing hands frequently may protect you against the bacteria and the virus. On the other hand, getting into the habit of smoking cigarettes and drinking alcohol too often may lead to severe cancer, and an unhealthy diet will increase the possibility of hypertension. If you form healthy habits, your body will never come to terms with illnesses. For another thing, it is worth highlighting that a positive spirit is also able to lower our risk of getting in hospital. Studies are there, indicating the benefit of being positive. One with a positive mood will be more likely to be free from diseases than those continuously aroused by a depressed and helplessness feeling.
In a nutshell, a number of factors may be relevant to our well-being, of which good living habits and optimistic mental position are of grand importance to our own health. Having these in mind, individuals can take more effective actions to optimize their health and well-being.
